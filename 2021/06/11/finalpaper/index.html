Down To Earth

Created2021-06-11|Updated2021-08-26|Share
|Post View:|Comments:

1092 實用科技英文

Final Paper

“Down to Earth”, a song that published in 2009 in the album which called My World and was certified platinum in the United States. Sung by Justin Bieber, who was only 16 to start his first world tour concert in 2010, and made his ticket sold out in 30 seconds is one of my favorite songs. It’s no need any gorgeous stage effect, but a piano and a vocal can make everyone in tears. I love the song not only the lyrics and melody are simple but moving, but also the meaning behind the song is very deep and touching, and even more the song always makes me recall those old times.
I remembered that the first time I was appealed by the song was due to the simple but powerful lyrics. When I was a kid, my mom often sung the song that its chorus keeps repeating the word “baby”. Due to my curiosity, I searched for the singer of the song, and I got to know that it was Justin Bieber. Since I started to own a cellphone, I have been listened to his album. I found the song “Down to Earth” in a playlist unexpectedly. At the first time I listened to this song, my thought was only that the melody is beautiful and nothing more. However, since I listened more and more times, I became more and more focused on the lyrics. “So tell me how could I ever be normal somehow” (lyrics of “Down to Earth”), it was not until I saw this sentence that I realized it was a gloomy song. The lyrics were straight, there is no pretty words, and the chord of the song didn’t have many changes. The song can make everyone in tears on the million people concert with only three chords and a vocal, that is, a piano and a microphone. The only things he needs to do is to sing the song out from the bottom of his heart. This is the song that Justin Bieber wrote about his family, and he had sung the song in tears once during singing.
This song is deep and it conveys a lot of his thoughts and the things that he wanted to express while his family was not completed behind it. Justin Bieber’s parents were divorced when his was young, and he was just a kid that hoped his family could be completed like everyone hoped at that time. I think that it must be very desperate of him to write such plain but powerful lyrics. Almost every time when I listened to this song, I would be silent and felt the lyrics. “It’s a ballad about the feelings I had when my parents split up and how I helped my family get through it. I think a lot of kids have had their parents split up, and they should know that it wasn’t because of something they did. I hope people can relate to it.”(an interview in November 14, 2009 issue of Billboard). He wanted to tell those children who have the same experience as him that “that it wasn’t because of something they did”, it’s not because of them that their family broke. He didn’t conceal his family, instead his let it be open, and expressed his real emotion frankly, I think it would be one of the reasons that why he named the song “Down to earth”. I really loved this song and it has been accompanied me to spend a lot of good time. When I listen to this song, it often reminds me of the past memory, such as the times that on my way to my mom’s company.
Whenever I listened to this song, I often recalled the childhood that I walked alone to my mom’s company. I remembered when I was in junior high school, I listened to music and ate some snacks on the way to my mom’s company after school. It’s really a good time that I can make myself relaxed and pleased. At that time, he sang the song with his voice that hadn’t changed, but he has a rougher voice after changing his voice now. The boy who started singing on the stage with a purple cap became the one who has his own wife has accompanied me through my childhood. Although he still sang “Down to Earth” from time to time, his voice was not that pure as first. His pure voice in the song can always bring me back to a certain point of my childhood, so I can feel the passage of time actually.
The song “Down to Earth” is really plain but touching. It not only has a deep meaning behind it and gives everyone food for thought, but made me recall those good times as well. It officially certified gold in the US in September, 2015. Talking about Justin Bieber’s songs without those honor, he has released many albums, songs since his first debut. Rather than those songs that everyone already knows and can sing without second thought, I love the song “Down to Earth”, which he sings with his pure voice and plain accompaniment more.
